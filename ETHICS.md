# ETHICS, PRIVACY & USAGE GUIDELINES

## Purpose

This repository is a research prototype to explore how microbiome profiles may help predict cancer treatment adverse events (AEs). It is **not** for clinical use.

## Data

- **No real patient data** is included in this repository. Only synthetic/demo data are provided.
- If you use real patient data, obtain IRB/Ethics approval, informed consent, and Data Transfer Agreements (DTA).
- Protect PHI and genomic data; do not publish raw sequences or identifiable metadata in public repos.

## Usage

- Model outputs are advisory and must not replace clinician judgement.
- Any prospective use in patients requires regulatory approvals and clinical trial validation.
